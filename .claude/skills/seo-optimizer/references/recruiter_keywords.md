## Keyword Optimization Strategy

### Primary Keyword Selection
1. **Choose 2-3 primary keywords** based on:
   - Your strongest technical skills
   - Your experience level
   - Your target location/work type
   - Current market demand

### Secondary Keywords
1. **Include 5-7 secondary keywords** that:
   - Support your primary keywords
   - Cover related technologies
   - Match recruiter search variations
   - Include location and experience modifiers

### Content Integration
```markdown
### Homepage Content Strategy
- Primary keyword in title tag and H1
- Secondary keywords in H2-H6 headings
- Natural integration in body content
- Location keywords in contact section
- Experience level in about section

### Project Pages Strategy
- Technology keywords in project descriptions
- Problem-solving keywords in challenges section
- Business impact keywords in results section
- Team collaboration keywords if applicable
```

## Keyword Density Guidelines

### Recommended Density Ranges
- **Primary keywords**: 1-2% of total words
- **Secondary keywords**: 0.5-1% of total words
- **Total keyword density**: 2-4% maximum
